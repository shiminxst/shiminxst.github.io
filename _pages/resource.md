Curated Medical Datasets
======

- **Harvard-FairVision30k:** Largest 2D (SLO fundus) + 3D (OCT Bscans) medical imaging fairness dataset (30,000 patients), involving glaucoma, age-related macular degeneration (AMD)  and diabeitic diabetic retinopathy (DR) diseases. Patient demographic information include gender, race, ethnicity, preferred lanague, marital status. [Download the Dataset](http://ophai.hms.harvard.edu/datasets/harvard-fairvision30k)
